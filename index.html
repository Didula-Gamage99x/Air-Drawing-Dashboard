How it works

Your webcam runs entirely in the browser. The app tracks your hand and turns your gestures into live drawing actions.

Tips

Keep your hand visible and well-lit. A steady hand and a clean background improve tracking accuracy noticeably.

Privacy

No drawings are sent anywhere. All processing stays on your device.
